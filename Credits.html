SkypeMenuX provides a System Extra menu for Skype.

This menu allows you to:

  • View and change your Skype user status
  • Launch or Quit Skype

Help?

For help, such as how to make SkypeMenuX launch automatically at startup, see the installation instructions.

The next version of this menu will allow you to:

  • See which of your Skype buddies are online
  • Start an IM or voice call to one of your online Skype buddies
